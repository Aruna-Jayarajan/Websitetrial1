Insignia's Motto

We are a group of engineering students from KJSCE sharing a passion for art and creativity. Insignia started in 2015 when a group of art lover got together to create something the college had never seen before. The team has experimented with various types of creative art expression like- string art, spray paints and now the team has taken a new turn to digital art.

Insignia and art

From then till now, insignia broke so many boundaries there was to expressing art. From paintings as long as 20 ft, to a batmobile, robot and even a castle, Insignia always finds a way to surprise its crowd. Till now Insignia has participated in KJSCEs college fests like Abhiyantriki(Technical fest) , Symphony(Cultural fest), Naari(Women’s day celebration),Freshers Eve and Farewell to the BTech students. Apart from this, Insignia is also known to be the creative partner of Parvaah-A students’ initiative, TEDXSomaiya Vidhyavihar, BioRIIDL and Melange. Team Insignia strives to create new forms of art and break the convention.
